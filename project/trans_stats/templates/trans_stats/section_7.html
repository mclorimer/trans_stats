VII. Location

The sample included respondents from all 50 states, the District of Columbia, American Samoa, Guam, Puerto Rico, and several U.S. military bases overseas. The geographic distribution of the sample generally mirrors that of the U.S. general population (Figure 4.19). The sample was divided into regions based on the Census Bureau regions, which included the Northeast, Midwest, South, and West (Figure 4.20). These regional categories did not include U.S. territories or U.S. military bases overseas.
